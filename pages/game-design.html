Game Design

Explore

the in-between, an etheral land somewhere between life and death shrouded in mystery and danger. Light up your path as you push your way through the darkness.

Collect

sheep to save them from the darkness. The more sheep you have with you, the more powerful you become. Be careful not to take too many with you, in case the wolves find you...

Protect

your flock from the wolves that come from the darkness. Be careful, if your sheep are eaten by the wolves, there is no way to get them back.

Become the Reaper

and attack the wolves, taking your revenge for your flock that was lost. Each wolf you kill will give you an extra sheep, allowing you to progress further into the unkown.
